Welcome to Planetary Express

Welcome to Planetary Express your intergalactic space faring delivery service where no job is too dangerous! For 10 years we have prided ourselves on being Earths number one space delivery service. It all started in 2011 when the worlds biggest superpowers finished the International Space Station, a wonderful vessel for stationing Earths bravest astronauts for the advancing of scientific research into the wonders of Space. But all that research left quite a few hungry astronauts who were sick of the boring and repetitive freeze dried foods and snacks. So Planetary Express and its founding crew delivered Earths first space faring pizza to the International Space Station. And ever since that humble beginning, PLanetary Express has grown into the Milky Ways greatest, most reliable and bravest Intergalactic Delivery Service the universe has ever seen! Delivering to planets far and wide from Mercury to Neptune and beyond!
