Welcome to the Halo Infinite Hype Page!

This web page is dedicated to the joy that we all felt seeing the Halo Infinite trailers and every press release leading up to the December 2021 release date! This installment of the most iconic multiplayer series of all time is returning to the roots of what made us fall in love with the halo series. And this page is all about highlighting the exciting things coming to Halo Infinite that let us relive the games before it in a new way.

Learn About Halos Triumphant Return!

Ever since the first teaser trailer in 2018, every Halo fan around the world could tell that the wonderful team at 343 industries had decided to return to the roots of Halo. Master Chief in his Mark IV armor from Halo 2 ignited a flame in hearts across the world for one reason, Halo was back! Here on the Fan Page you'll find resources to get you hyped for Halos return to the gaming scene and some fun art and images around the world building of Halo Infinite.
